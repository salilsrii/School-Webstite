Amrit Public School started in 2007, presently one of the leading school of mau. It has the best infrastructure in terms of highly techno savy classrooms, well equipped library, well experienced staff, trained counselors and affiliated for 10+2 from Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. Amrit Public School is a progressive, co-educational school, committed to providing best education for all its students. Amrit Public School has a qualified, experienced & dedicated team of teachers to impart quality education. We give importance to the quality education & enviroment in the school. Amrit Public School is committed for providing the education in terms of all round development of the personality of students as per C.B.S.E. Board.
